Glitch Art

Tool(s): Notepad+, Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: In this project, I used a text editor to glitch this image. Then, I used Photoshop to cut out parts of the glitched image and put it onto the normal image and make a collage. I used the rectangular marque tool to cut out various rectangular shapes and arranged them in a grid-like pattern.
