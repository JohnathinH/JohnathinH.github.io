New Species Design

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: I made this image by having a sheep as the base image, and I used a dog for the face, an elephant's trunk for the tail, and a gorilla to replace the front part of the arms. I used adjustment layers to adjust colors and make the other animals blend into the base images more. I used a soft edge brush to feather out edges and make the images blend into eachother. I also used Photoshop's AI autofill tool to extend the background.
