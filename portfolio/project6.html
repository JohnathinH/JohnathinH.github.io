Book Cover

Tool(s): ChatGPT, Adobe Firefly AI, Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: I used this by first using ChatGPT to generate a title and subtitle for a "Lord of the Rings" like book. Then, I used it again to give me a prompt for generating a book cover. I then input that prompt into Adobe Firefly's text-to-image AI, and got the image seen below. I edited the image to be book cover proportions and used Photoshop to edit text on top to finish the cover.
